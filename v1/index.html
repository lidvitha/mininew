Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known astronomical object to harbor life.

Earth's Species

Earth is home to millions of species, including humans, and has a diverse range of ecosystems.

Earth's Atmosphere

Earth's atmosphere protects life on the planet by absorbing ultraviolet solar radiation.

Earth's Water Content

Approximately 71% of Earth's surface is covered with water, most of which is found in the oceans. Freshwater is vital for life on land.

Human Life on Earth

Human life on Earth depends on the planet's natural resources, including water, air, and a stable climate. Humans have adapted to a wide range of environments on Earth.
